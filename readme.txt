Required mods - 
F4se - http://f4se.silverlock.org/
MCM - https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/21497/

Install Instructions - 

MAKE SURE YOU HAVE ENABLED MODDING IN YOUR GAME - http://wiki.tesnexus.com/index.php/Fallout_4_Mod_Installation

1a. If you use the Unofficial Fallout 4 Patch, move 00 uf4p/ConfigurablePowerArmor.esp into your Data folder.
1b. Otherwise, move 01 vanilla/ConfigurablePowerArmor.esp into your Data folder.

2. Copy MCM and Scripts folders into your data folder

3. Enable the mod and play!
